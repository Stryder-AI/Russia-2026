Russian Visa

Visa Requirements

Here's everything you need to prepare. We arrange the rest — including the official invitation letter and MoFA attestation.

What You Provide

  1. Machine-readable passport, valid for a minimum of 6 months, with two blank pages.
  2. Copies of any previous passports (if a passport was lost, a police report will be required).
  3. Latest colour photograph on a white background — 3.5 × 4.5 cm.
  4. Police Character Certificate, duly attested by MoFA (we arrange the attestation).
  5. Family Registration Certificate.
  6. Employment certificate stating the applicant's position and duration of employment, plus proof of income — last 3 months' salary slips.
    If self-employed: business letterhead (original) with business stamp.
  7. Account maintenance certificate and bank statement of the last 6 months.

What We Arrange (Included)

  • Flight reservations and paid hotel-accommodation confirmation (arranged after receipt of your payment).
  • Original invitation letter from the concerned ministry of Russia.
  • MoFA attestation of your Police Character Certificate.
  • Full visa application & processing.
  • Health Insurance.
